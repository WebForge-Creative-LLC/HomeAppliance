Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to the most common questions about our products and services.

What types of commercial bathroom equipment do you supply?

We supply a comprehensive range of commercial bathroom equipment including handwash sinks, hands-free faucets, soap dispensers, commercial drains, shower systems, and more. Our product catalog covers everything from basic fixtures to advanced smart solutions.

Do you provide installation services?

Yes, we offer professional installation services for all our products. Our certified technicians ensure proper installation and provide comprehensive training for maintenance staff. We also offer ongoing maintenance and support services.

What is your typical response time for inquiries?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent requests, we offer expedited support through our hotline. Our live chat feature provides instant answers to common questions during business hours.

Do you offer custom solutions for specific requirements?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom solutions tailored to your specific needs. Our engineering team works closely with clients to design and manufacture products that meet unique specifications, compliance requirements, and design preferences.

What warranty do you provide on your products?

All our products come with comprehensive warranties ranging from 1-5 years depending on the product type. We also offer extended warranty options and our products are backed by our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Can you help with compliance and certification requirements?

Yes, we have extensive experience with various compliance standards including ADA, NSF, WaterSense, and local building codes. Our team can guide you through certification processes and ensure your project meets all regulatory requirements.
