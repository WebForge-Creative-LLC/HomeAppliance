Our Journey

The AquaFlow Story

Founded in 1995, AquaFlow Solutions began with a simple mission: to provide the highest quality commercial bathroom equipment that combines innovation, durability, and exceptional performance.

1995 - The Beginning

Founded with a vision to revolutionize commercial bathroom solutions with cutting-edge technology and superior quality.

2005 - Industry Recognition

Achieved ISO 9001 certification and received our first industry award for innovation in commercial bathroom equipment.

2015 - Global Expansion

Expanded operations globally, serving clients across North America, Europe, and Asia with our premium solutions.

2024 - Industry Leader

Recognized as a leading supplier of commercial bathroom equipment, serving over 5000 satisfied clients worldwide.
