Touchless Faucet Systems

Our hands-free faucet systems represent the pinnacle of modern bathroom technology, combining advanced infrared sensor technology with elegant design. These touchless faucets provide enhanced hygiene, water conservation, and user convenience in high-traffic commercial environments.

Perfect for healthcare facilities, food service operations, and public spaces where hygiene is paramount. Our sensor faucets feature reliable operation, adjustable settings, and energy-efficient design for optimal performance and cost savings.

Key Features

  • Advanced infrared sensor technology
  • Battery and AC powered options
  • Adjustable flow control
  • Automatic shut-off functionality
  • Water-saving design
  • Easy installation and maintenance
  • ADA compliant models available
  • 5-year warranty on materials and workmanship

Specifications

Sensor Type: Infrared
Power Source: Battery or AC
Flow Rate: 0.5-1.5 GPM
Detection Range: 2-6 inches
Battery Life: 2-5 years
